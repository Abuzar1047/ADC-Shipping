Cargo Aircraft Charter

ADC Air Cargo is the world leader in the specialist market of Live Animal Air Transportation.

 

We are highly experienced in creating the correct cargo cabin conditions to ensure the safe carriage of live animals.

 

Using the modern fleets of aircraft witch is very well equipped and our crews have experience in managing livestock cargo.
Clients are assured of a reliable and comprehensive service for the safe and efficient handling, transport and delivery of their livestock. 


The carriage of any live animal by air must comply with Animal Welfare and the current edition of the IATA Live Animals Regulations. These regulations specify the design elements of the containers for the animals, documentation, labeling and handling recommendations.
