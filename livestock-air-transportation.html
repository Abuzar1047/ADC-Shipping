Livestock Air Transportation

Livestock Cargo has special needs, ADC Air Cargo assure you that your animals would travel in comfort and safety onboard in our temperature-controlled for A330-200/300F, B777-300F & B747-400F.html freighters.

 

Our staff is trained to current IATA Live Animals Regulations (LARs), to ensure that your animals are handled and transported with care, safely and providing minimum stress.

 

The flight is escorted by our livestock attendant who takes care for the health and welfare of the animals during the flight and all loading activities.
